Legend & Critical Flow Rules

Checkout authorityThe browser sends intent only. Price, delivery fee, discount, stock, and payment state are validated server-side.
Buy Now flowBuy Now creates DirectCheckoutSessionDO and does not mutate the normal CartDO cart.
Inventory authorityOnline checkout and POS stock deduction both go through VariantInventoryDO.
Payment webhooksWebhook payloads are signature-verified, queued, then verified server-to-server before D1 payment status changes.
Staff protectionStaff and POS routes are protected by Zero Trust Access, RBAC middleware, CSRF protection, and audit logs.
Async reliabilityEmails, image processing, fraud audit, cart activity, backups, and reconciliation run through queues and cron.
